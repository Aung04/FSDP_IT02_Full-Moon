About the Workshop

Master public speaking and inspire with confidence!

Master key techniques to engage audiences, craft compelling messages, and overcome stage fear. In this upcoming workshop led by Mr. Alan Lim, attendees will practice speech delivery techniques, including speech structure, delivery styles, projection, and connection. The workshop offers practical exercises and personalized feedback, making it ideal for beginners and those looking to refine their public speaking skills.
